One Piece

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with a rubber body

after eating a Devil Fruit.He sets out on a journey to find the legendary

treasure, One Piece,and become the Pirate King. Along the way,he

gathers a diverse crew,faces powerful enemies, and uncovers the world's

hidden secrets.Their adventure takes them across dangerous seas,

mysterious islands,and epic battles.


Naruto

respect.He trains hard,makes friends,and battles powerful enemies.Along the

Solo Levelling

Solo Leveling follows Jinwoo Sung,a weak hunter in a world where humans

fight monsters in dungeons.After a near-death experience,he gains a unique

ability to level up infinitely. He grows stronger, uncovering hidden truths

about the dungeons and his destiny.

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a boy whose family is slaughtered by

demons, with his sister Nezuko being turned into one. Determined to save

her and avenge his family, he joins the Demon Slayer Corps. He battles

powerful demons while uncovering the secrets behind their existence.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who swallows

a cursed object and becomes the host of a powerful curse, Sukuna.To

control this power, he joins Jujutsu High, where sorcerers battle curses

that threaten humanity. Yuji and his friends fight dangerous curses while

uncovering dark secrets of the sorcerer world.


Bleach

Bleach follows Ichigo Kurosaki,a teenager who gains Soul Reaper powers

after meeting Rukia Kuchiki.He battles evil spirits called Hollows and

protects both the human and spirit worlds.As he grows stronger,he faces

powerful enemies and uncovers hidden truths about his past.

Dragon Ball Z

Dragon Ball Z follows Goku, a powerful Saiyan warrior, as he defends Earth

from formidable enemies. Alongside his friends, he battles villains like

Frieza, Cell, and Majin Buu while constantly training to become stronger.

The series features intense fights, epic transformations, and the pursuit

of ultimate power.

Attack On Titan

Attack on Titan follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they fight against

gigantic humanoid Titans that threaten humanity.As they uncover hidden

truths about the Titans and their own world,they face intense battles

and moral dilemmas.The story explores survival, freedom, and the cost of war.

Tokyo Ghoul

Tokyo Ghoul follows Ken Kaneki,a college student who becomes a half
